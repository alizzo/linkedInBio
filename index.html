Philosophies

Design Philosophy: Our design philosophy revolves around simplified user interfaces, which are backed by elegance, fast, and complex logic. This combination of concepts creates an intuitive design that is aesthetically pleasing, yet dynamic in functionality. Simply put, our products are easy to use, and they will solve your needs.

Core Values: Here at Da Vinci solutions, we want to channel the energy of Leonardo himself by creating aesthetically beautiful designs that innovate while solving problems of all types. As Da Vinci himself was a creator, inventor, scientist, and artist, we want to center all celebrations of human brilliance in our design solutions. We highly value feedback, consistency, and efficiency in our programs while designing systems that will excite people.

Problem Solving Approach: Our approach to problem solving can take different perspectives depending on the scope, complexity, and subject matter. Typically, Da Vinci Design implements best paradigms, visions, theories, models, and frameworks, but other times we think outside of the box. Our organization has a variety of different brainstorming techniques that lead to our best ideas and astray from worst ideas. We offer a variety of elicitation techniques such as surveys, interviews, role play, etc... For more simple projects, our organization might utilize the Design Council of the UK Double Diamond Approach, as you can imagine more complex problems require more research, user input, concept modeling, and concept prototypes. Additionally, we employ the use of artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques where applicable to optimize systems.
